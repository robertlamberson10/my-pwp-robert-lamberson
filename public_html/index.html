•My Story•

I am a recent graduate from Amarillo College's first web development bootcamp. I am looking to pursue development and coding full time. As I hone my skills and capabilities in the field, I hope to continue developing web pages while becoming more adept in blockchain technology and development. My time spent traveling as a locomotive technician for the US Army made me realize I wanted to pursue my interests in the development world with the hope of staying closer to my family. I grew up in Amarillo and now my wife and I are raising our two kids Luke and Olivia here as well. When I am not developing or chasing kids I enjoy drag racing and most sports with an emphasis on football.

I recently completed a Full Stack coding bootcamp at Amarillo College. In the 10-week course I worked on code for 50+ hours a week every week. As part of my bootcamp I took part in a three-person collaboration project. Learning how to develop with a team and communicating using GitHub was a big emphasis for me. Our collaboration led us to creating a much-needed hiring site for local Amarillo tech talent in only six weeks. I also created a personal work portfolio (which happens to be this page) to not only get information about my self and my projects but also display another development project I completed in the 10-week course.

Purpose, Audience, and Goal

Purpose

This project is geared to building an online portfolio for myself with focus on my skills and hopes of garnering attention from potential employers or clients.

Audience

This website is for future employers and clients. The target audience is professionals in the Web development and technology field all over the country.

Goal

The goal of this website is to get in contact with users looking to hire or contract my services. I also hope to display my personal work for users of the site and develop an online presence.

Persona

Name

Randy Young

Age

40

Profession

Hiring manager for Techdesign.com, an established tech company looking to hire remote full stack Developers. They have just landed a bunch of new clients and are looking to add to their team of remote web developers to fill their new customers' web development needs.

Technology

Windows and Android power user. His work computer is a Dell Precision 7920 with a Dell ultra sharp 4K 29" monitor. His laptop at home is a Surface laptop 4 with a 13.5" touchscreen monitor. His cell phone is a Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra 5G.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Randy has a busy work schedule and is generally pressed for time. He looks at multiple work portfolios a day trying to fill positions. He looks for someone that stands out and is organized with their resume and portfolio. He uses a hiring website for some candidates but loves to search Dice.com and LinkedIn for potential employees.

Frustrations and Needs

Randy is looking to hire for 3 positions. All of these are entry level positions. The company needs someone proficient with Github and who works well in a team setting. Because most projects are built by a team of developers, the right chemistry is vital for success. The company offers a mid-range salary but has great 401k match and other benefits. Techdesign.com is needing to hire people as soon as possible to keep up with their customers' needs.

Goals

Randy needs to hire multiple people who require minimal training. Since Randy has many portfolios to review, he spends the majority of his time looking for the specific technical and interpersonal skills that meet his hiring requirements. The job listing was recently posted and has had multiple applicants. Randy needs to narrow candidates as quickly as possible.

User Story

As a hiring manager, it is essential to identify the best candidates for permanent full stack positions in a timely manner.

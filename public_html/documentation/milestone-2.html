Content Strategy

Navbar

In this section I will have a navbar with clickable links to all content on page. Also I will have a link to my resume. There will also be a clickable home link that is my initials in the top left corner. ALl of this will be opaque with just the words or letter themselves displayed

Welcome

This will be where a brief introduction to me and the page will be displayed. Next to the text will be a picture of myself at the top of the page under the navbar.

About Me

Here is where you will get a brief description of me and my skills as well as a history of my personal business history. Also it will display my strengths and some other details that inform the employer on the person they are trying to.

Portfolio

In this area of the page I will display all my work to this point with a brief description of the work and the tools I used in the process. There will be pictures displayed with the links to the sites themselves as well as some styling. There will also be a section describing the tools I know and are proficient in with logo pictures of the tools and some added style.

Contact Me

In this footer there will be a contact me section that is interactive. There will also be the clickable links to all of my social media accounts.
